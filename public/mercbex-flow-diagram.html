01 — Overview: End-to-End Flow

Summary: The user selects a crop on the home page, scans it via camera or upload, gets an AI-powered diagnosis with reference images for comparison, confirms the diagnosis, then receives medicine recommendations they can add to cart with full scan context.

04 — Status Definitions

SAVED (blue)

Farmer saved the diagnosis but hasn't confirmed treatment yet.

  • Farmer compared reference images and agreed the diagnosis looks right
  • Clicked "Save Diagnosis" — persisted to My Diagnoses
  • "Confirm & See Treatment" button now visible but not yet clicked
  • Can come back later to confirm and see medicines

ACTION NEEDED (red)

Farmer confirmed treatment view but no medicines added to cart yet.

  • Saved the diagnosis AND clicked "Confirm & See Treatment"
  • Medicine recommendations are visible
  • Medicines NOT yet added to cart
  • Knows the problem, hasn't started purchasing

IN CART (amber)

Confirmed AND medicines added to cart.

  • Full flow completed: Save → Confirm → Add to Cart
  • Recommended medicines added with quantity
  • Purchase still pending (not checked out)
  • Cart shows crop + disease context

NOT SAVED

Farmer left before saving. Diagnosis not persisted to history.

  • Scan completed, results shown
  • Farmer didn't click Save or Reject
  • Session preserved in localStorage for resume
  • Does NOT appear in My Diagnoses

Cart Grouping Logic

  Cart items are grouped by crop:

  1. Extract unique crops from all cart items with scanContext
  2. Group items under each crop header
  3. Show crop icon + name as section header
  4. Under each item, show: disease name + scan time
